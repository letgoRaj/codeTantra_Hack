ABOUT US

KALIPEELI

KaliPeeli Carpool is a pioneering carpooling platform that is revolutionizing the way people commute, efficiently connecting individuals traveling in the same direction. Inspired by the iconic "Kali Peeli" taxis synonymous with shared transportation in many cities, we strive to provide a modern, eco-friendly alternative to solo driving.

Our mission at KaliPeeli Carpool is to optimize daily commutes, promote cost-effective travel, and significantly reduce the overall carbon footprint. We are dedicated to creating a seamless, reliable, and sustainable carpooling experience that benefits both commuters and the environment.
